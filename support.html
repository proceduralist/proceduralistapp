Support

We're here to help.

For questions, bug reports, content corrections, or feature requests, email us at:

proceduralist@outlook.com

We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible.

When Contacting Us, Please Include

  • Your device model and iOS version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18);
  • The version of Proceduralist you're using;
  • A description of the issue and, if relevant, the procedure or screen where it occurred;
  • Screenshots, if helpful.

Found a Content Error?

Medical accuracy matters to us. If you believe any supply list, procedural step, contraindication, complication, or reference is incorrect or outdated, please let us know — include the procedure name and the specific item in question, along with a supporting citation if available.

Common Questions

Is my portfolio data backed up anywhere?
Portfolio entries and preferences are stored only on your device. They are not synced to any server. If you delete the App, this data is deleted with it (unless included in your own device backup).

Does the App work offline?
Yes. All content, including videos, is bundled with the App and available without an internet connection.

Can the App's steps replace my hospital's protocol?
No. The App is an educational reference only. Always follow your institution's policies and protocols. See our Terms of Use.
