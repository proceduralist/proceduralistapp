Proceduralist is a comprehensive reference tool that provides clinicians with standard equipment lists, step-by-step procedural reviews, evidence-based literature, and a personal logbook to track their own procedural history.

What's Inside

Four tabs. Quick access to supply lists, procedural reviews, evidence-based references, and a personal logbook.

🧰 Supplies

Complete supply lists with optional kit checklists for each procedure.

📋 Steps

Clear, step-by-step walkthroughs from preparation to post procedure care.

🎥 Procedure Image and Videos

Visual aids and reference media to review key procedural steps.

⚠️ Indications, Contraindications & Complications

Quickly review indications, absolute and relative contraindications, and complications for each procedure.

📁 Portfolio

Log the procedures you perform and build a personal portfolio over time.

📚 References

Browse full citations from journals like NEJM, JAMA, CHEST, and Anesthesiology directly in the app.

Try It Yourself

Interactive previews of the app screens below.

Supplies — browse procedures, then check off every item. Try the Lumbar Puncture list.

Steps — filter by category and open Lumbar Puncture to expand each procedure step.

Clinical Info — indications, contraindications, and complications (tap for details).

Portfolio — tap + and − to log procedures; every entry is date-stamped in your log history.
