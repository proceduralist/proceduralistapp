What's Inside

Four tabs. Everything you need before, during, and after the procedure.

🧰 Supplies

Complete supply and kit checklists for every procedure, including common commercial kits. Customize kit preferences to match what your institution stocks so you never walk into the room missing an item.

📋 Steps

Clear, step-by-step walkthroughs from positioning to dressing, with embedded video demonstrations of key maneuvers — ultrasound technique, needle handling, manometry, and more.

⚠️ Indications, Contraindications & Complications

Quickly review indications, absolute and relative contraindications, and complications for each procedure — with the supporting level of evidence graded so you know how strong the data is.

📁 Portfolio

Log the procedures you perform and build a personal portfolio over time — useful for credentialing and tracking your own experience. Stored privately on your device only.

📚 References

Every recommendation is backed by peer-reviewed literature. Browse full citations from journals like NEJM, JAMA, CHEST, and Anesthesiology directly in the app.

🎥 Procedure Videos

Short, focused video clips demonstrate the hardest parts of each procedure — from arterial line draping and suturing to lumbar puncture manometry and stylet handling.

Try It Yourself

Live, interactive previews using real content from the app — tap, check, and swipe through a lumbar puncture.
